Open Doors to the Land of Opportunities: Student Visa to the USA

The USA is a dream for many, and we are here to help you turn that dream into reality. A student visa to the USA is your key to education, new connections, and a bright future

The USA is the most coveted country worldwide, and we understand why. Here, every day is an opportunity for personal and professional growth. We're with you every step of the way to make your journey to the USA easy and successful.

Opening a Student Visa: We offer the option to obtain a student visa through college enrollment. You can choose a major that interests you or take English language courses at the college to prepare for the next stage of your life.

Important!

For those who do not want to study but simply wish to get a visa and travel to the USA, we have good news. There's no need to pay for the full tuition or the entire English language course. Paying for the first month is sufficient!

Official Fees:

  • 160$ - Consular Fee
  • 300$ - SEVIS Fee
  • From $250 a week - For Tuition

We aim to make your experience of obtaining a visa and studying in the USA enjoyable and unforgettable. Trust us, and let's begin your adventure in the most exciting country in the world. Forward to new horizons!
