Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose your company?

You don't have to contact us specifically, you can contact any other company you like. However, if you decide to trust us, we'll do everything to ensure your satisfaction!
We have been operating efficiently in the market for 7 years, employing proprietary methodologies based on analytical research, whether it's obtaining visas or immigration, education, or starting your own business.
Each of our clients receives comprehensive support, from assistance in filling out documents to finding employers, selecting the best educational programs, relocation, settling in, and business establishment.

How are the services paid for?

Our operations are entirely transparent; all services are paid based on issued invoices and the signing of a legal agreement certified by a European notary. Payment is made to the company's bank account. You are fully protected by the law!

How much do your services cost?

It depends on the service you choose, whether it's work, education, or opening a business in Europe. Our prices align with the level of services provided. Qualified specialists will accompany you, and we conduct a thorough analysis of your chances, asking additional questions, examining your past and present, offering personalized solutions, and adapting the situation to suit you.

Do you provide guarantees?

We guarantee the high-quality execution of our services, full immersion in your situation, and an individual approach. This significantly increases your chances of a positive resolution to your request. The final decision on your request is always made by the consul and other competent authorities.
Regardless of the service you order from us, we enter into a contract that protects your interests and use only proven and legal methods of operation.

I have another question. Where do I find the answer?

If you haven't found the answer to your question, please feel free to contact us via WhatsApp or Telegram. Our manager will be happy to assist you with any inquiries you may have.
